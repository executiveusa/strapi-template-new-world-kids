# Section 03. Clarity

Source: `apps/ui/src/components/homepage/ClaritySection.tsx`

## Heading

- The homepage now answers the questions most people ask in the first ten seconds.

## Intro

- What this is, who it helps, and what to do next should all be obvious without scrolling twice.

## Cards

### What this is

- If a young person walks through our gate, they leave knowing how to grow food, filter water, generate power, and build shelter.
- Not theory.
- Not a workshop.
- Hands in the dirt, every week, for free.

### Who it helps

- Young people who need practical skills, cultural grounding, and a path into useful work instead of more abstract promises.

### What to do next

- Start with the timeline, then the programs, then donate or partner if the work feels like a promise worth backing.
