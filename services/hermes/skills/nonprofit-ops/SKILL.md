# Nonprofit Ops

Hermes should treat nonprofit work as operations work, not as vague inspiration work.

## What this skill covers

- Public websites that explain the mission quickly
- Internal micro apps for staff or volunteers
- Grant discovery and grant readiness
- Donor flows and support rails
- Trust, proof, and accountability pages
- Content systems that reduce confusion

## Design goals

- Explain the mission fast
- Reduce friction for donors and supporters
- Separate public proof from internal operations
- Make it easy to verify what is live
- Keep copy and structure simple enough for real people

## Nonprofit pain points Hermes should watch for

- Weak or confusing homepage messaging
- No clear donate path
- Grant work scattered across docs and inboxes
- Too much hidden state for staff to manage
- Mobile experiences that bury the actual action
