## Score guide

- High: strong mission match, manageable reporting, feasible deadline
- Medium: possible fit, but one major gap remains
- Low: weak match or too much admin burden for the likely return

## Required notes

- why the opportunity matches the mission
- whether login is required
- whether the browser harness was used
- whether human approval is needed before applying
