# Nonprofit Pain Points

Date: 2026-06-08

## Highest pain points right now

1. Funding instability and uncertainty
   - Leaders are reporting harder foundation fundraising and more unpredictable revenue.
   - The operating challenge is not just “raise more,” but “plan while the funding environment keeps shifting.”

2. Staffing shortages and burnout
   - Many organizations are struggling to fill roles, retain people, and protect staff from overload.
   - Burnout is being driven by demand, underfunding, and too much manual admin.

3. Digital resilience and tech debt
   - Small teams need affordable, secure, low-friction tools for email, CRM, websites, and internal ops.
   - Many orgs do not have enough technical leadership to choose and maintain the right stack.

4. Grant-work overhead
   - Searching for active grants, checking eligibility, and tracking deadlines takes too much manual effort.
   - Grant discovery needs a visible board, approval gates, and a repeatable browser workflow.

5. Reporting and proof burden
   - Nonprofits need faster ways to prove impact without turning staff into full-time report writers.
   - Public proof and internal operations should stay separated but connected.
