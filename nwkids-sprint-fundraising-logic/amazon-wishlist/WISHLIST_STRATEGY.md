## WHY AN AMAZON WISH LIST?

1. **Lower barrier to giving** - People who can't afford $100 cash might buy a $30 item
2. **Tangible impact** - Donors see exactly what their money buys
3. **Christmas timing** - People are already in gift-giving mode
4. **Corporate gift cards** - Many companies give Amazon gift cards as bonuses
5. **Matching programs** - Some employers match product donations
