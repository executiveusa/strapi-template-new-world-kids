**"I need to think about it."**
"Of course. Can I send you some information? And when would be a good time to follow upâ€”next week?"

**"I'm not sure my company matches."**

### Close
"Thank you so much for considering this. I'll send you the donation link and instructions for submitting the match. If you have any questions, please reach out anytime."
