## WHY STEWARDSHIP MATTERS

**The goal:** Turn one-time donors into repeat donors and advocates.

**The math:**
- Cost to acquire new donor: $1.00+ per dollar raised
- Cost to retain existing donor: $0.20 per dollar raised
- Repeat donors give 40% more than first-time donors
- A donor retained for 5+ years is worth 10x a one-time donor

**The rule:** Thank within 48 hours. Update monthly. Ask once per quarter.

## KEY PRINCIPLES

### The 7:1 Rule
For every 1 ask, provide 7 touches of value (updates, thanks, stories, photos).

### The 48-Hour Rule
Thank every donor within 48 hours. No exceptions.

### The Specificity Rule
Don't say "your donation helps kids." Say "your $100 funded 4 fruit trees that will produce for 30 years."

### The Photo Rule
Every thank you should include a photo when possible. Visuals create connection.

### The Name Rule
Always use their first name. Never "Dear Donor" or "Dear Friend."

### The Honesty Rule
If something went wrong, tell them. Transparency builds trust.
