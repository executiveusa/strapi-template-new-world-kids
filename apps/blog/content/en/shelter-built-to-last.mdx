Shelter is where ideals either touch the ground or disappear.

If the physical environment is fragile, everything around it remains fragile too: the classes, the storage, the trust, the continuity. That is why the shelter pillar matters so much in the New World Kids story.

## What this pillar teaches

Shelter is not just a building outcome. It is also a curriculum.

The repo context points toward:

- adobe restoration
- dome assembly
- climate-appropriate building choices
- practical methods that can travel back into community life

This is what makes the shelter story stronger than a generic facilities campaign. The structure itself teaches.

## What donors want to know fast

People do not want to decode a wall of nonprofit language. They want the site to answer, quickly:

- what is being built
- why it matters
- what stage it is in
- whether the organization is credible

That is why the trust layer and the shelter story need to sit close to each other in the experience.

## What this pillar funds

Support on the shelter side helps move:

- repairs
- materials
- space readiness
- safety improvements
- the public documentation that shows progress honestly

## What a great version looks like

The best version of this page family is simple at the top and deep beneath it. A donor sees the point immediately. A careful reviewer can open the documents, read the journal, and understand the operating structure without leaving the site confused.

That is the bar.
