We do not want the mission to survive on one funding source alone.

That is why New World Kids pairs charitable support with earned revenue from media, design, and AI services. The point is not to confuse the two. The point is to make the organization more resilient.

## What donors should understand

Donations support the nonprofit mission directly.

Services create additional cash flow that helps keep the educational work free to students and less dependent on a single cycle of fundraising pressure.

## Why this belongs in public

Many nonprofits hide the business logic behind the mission because they worry it will sound complicated.

We think clarity is better. If people understand how the money mix works, they can decide whether they want to support the mission, hire the studio, or do both.

## Where this shows up on the site

The public site should make three things easy to distinguish:

- donate to the mission
- support creator-media work through Buy Me a Coffee
- hire the team for aligned AI or media services

That separation protects trust. It also makes the whole organization easier to understand.
