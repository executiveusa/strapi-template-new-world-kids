# Emad Mostaque: Key Ideas & Vision

## Core Identity
- **Founder:** The Intelligent Internet (II), founded June 2024
- **Background:** Former Founder & CEO of Stability AI
- **Location:** London
- **Focus:** Universal Basic AI (UBAI), decentralized AI infrastructure, sovereign intelligence

## Top Quotes & Central Concepts

### Universal Basic AI (UBAI)
> "Everyone should have the right to an AI that is open, aligned and sovereign to them, that's looking out for their flourishing."

**Core Idea:** UBAI as the foundation for equitable AI access—each person entitled to a personalized AI assistant independent of corporate or state control.

### The Three-Tier Architecture of Intelligent Internet

**Tier 1:** National hyper-scalable nodes for massive model training
- Foundation layer for computational power
- Ensures base model quality at scale

**Tier 2:** Domain-distributed nodes for local fine-tuning
- Specialized adaptations for regional/industry needs
- Maintains sovereignty and customization

**Tier 3:** AI assistants on edge devices (Raspberry Pi level)
- Privacy-preserving personal AI
- Sovereign computation at the individual level
- No central authority access

### SAGE (Sovereign AI Governance Engine)
- **Launched:** October 2025
- **Backing:** Saudi Future Investment Initiative
- **Function:** Agentic platform analyzing AI breakthroughs and generating impact simulations for policy decisions
- **Purpose:** Make AI governance scientific, data-driven, and decentralized

## Strategic Vision & Predictions

### The Economic Argument
> "GDP & Capitalism Is Obsolete in an AI World"

**Central Thesis:** Traditional economic models (GDP, capitalism) fundamentally misalign with AI-driven abundance. Need new frameworks for:
- Value distribution in post-scarcity scenarios
- Recognition of AI-generated wealth
- Individual agency in an AI-powered economy

### Timeline & Urgency
- **"900 Days Left"** suggests critical window for establishing sovereign AI infrastructure before centralized systems become entrenched
- **Timeframe:** 2024-2026 window for foundational decisions
