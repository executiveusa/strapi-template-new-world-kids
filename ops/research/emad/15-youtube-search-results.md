## Key Theme: Cognitive Colonialism (Summary)
Mostaque defines cognitive colonialism as the risk that AI systems built by Silicon Valley or Chinese tech giants impose their embedded values, morals, and worldviews on global users. His counter-proposal is open-source, sovereign AI (UBAI / II-Agent) funded by public compute rather than advertising revenue.
